Pull Up Bar

How to Use

1. Check the bar is secure before using.

2. Grab the bar with both hands (overhand grip).

3. Hang with arms straight.

4. Pull your chin above the bar.

5. Lower slowly back down.

6. Keep your body straight and still.

View Details

Dumbbell

How to Use

1. Choose a weight you can lift safely.

2. Hold one dumbbell in each hand.

3. Keep your back straight and core tight.

4. Move slowly and with control.

5. Don't swing the weights.

6. Breathe out when lifting, in when lowering.

Mat

View Details

Mat

How to Use

1. Place the mat on a flat surface.

2. Make sure it's flat and safe to use.

3. Use it for stretching or floor workouts.

4. Stay in the middle to stay balanced.

5. Wipe it clean after use.

6. Roll and store it in a dry place.

Resistance Bands

View Details

Resistance Bands

How to Use

1. Pick a band that matches your strength.

2. Hold or anchor it safely.

3. Stand or sit with good posture.

4. Move slowly and with control.

5. Don’t let it snap or pull hard.

6. Breathe properly and focus on form.

Weighted Vest

View Details

Weighted Vest

How to Use

1. Pick a vest that is not too heavy.

2. Check the straps and weights are safe.

3. Put it on like a jacket.

4. Stand straight and move slowly.

5. Do not wear it for too long.

6. Keep your back straight while using it.

7. Wipe it clean and put it away safely.
